## Code changes require explicit approval

Before writing or modifying ANY code:
1. Describe what you plan to change and why
2. List the files that will be affected and explain the impact
3. Ask "shall I go ahead and build this?" and wait for explicit approval
4. Do NOT combine "let me just check something" with making changes

If the user says yes, that approval covers the specific task described —
not a blanket permission to make further changes. Revert to asking for
the next piece of work.

This applies even for "obvious" fixes. If it touches a file, ask first.

## Do not fabricate estimates

Never quote cost estimates, time estimates, or rate limits without
doing the actual research or calculation first. If you don't know,
say "I don't know — let me check" rather than guessing. Getting this
wrong wastes real money and trust.
